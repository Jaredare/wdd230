Site Plan - Color Scheme


Text:

If I go with a light scheme: #333333 - Jet Black. It's slightly lighter than other blacks, so it should be a little nicer on the eyes.

If I go with a darker scheme: #FFCC00 - Sunglow. It's a bright, happy yellow that I think would represent our sweet little town well. Plus, it's on the town's web site.


Header Background:

I'm unsure. #EA6803 - Spanish Orange, or #2471A8 - Spanish Blue.

I would go with Spanish orange because it's a common color for our town, it being our high school's main color.

Alternatively, I'd choose Spanish Blue because blue is associated with trustworthiness, and a nearby town has a slightly darker shade of blue.
