Site Plan - Target Audience


Target Audience:

Visitors of Honey Grove, or people interested in Honey Grove's politics or economy. Generally these people would be up and coming business owners or big businesses wanting to establish a chain here. Tourists may also visit the site in hopes of finding something interesting.


Personas:

John is a 28 year old man with a wife who wants to visit a friend for an event. John wants to stay in the area for a weekend, and found the website because he wants to see what there is to visit around the area and see if there are any interesting local businesses.
Mary is 31 year old woman with a husband and 2 teenage kids who wants to start a business in the area. She wants to see if there will be competition for her business, as well as what kind of community events she can take her family to.


Scenarios

  • Where can I stay in Honey Grove? Would I be better off finding a bigger city nearby to stay in?
  • Are there any tourist attractions around here that I should visit before I leave?
  • What kind of businesses are here? Do they generally care about supporting the community?
  • Are there events in this town that I can take my family to?
  • Is my nephew really a member of the Honey Grove Chamber of Commerce?
